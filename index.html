What We Do

The infrastructure every autonomous fleet needs.

Autonomous vehicles don't just need roads — they need charging, cleaning, and servicing hubs embedded in the cities they operate in. That's what Flock builds and operates, partnered inside existing parkade infrastructure across Canada's major urban centres.

We partner with parkade operators — not compete with them. Flock absorbs all permit and electrical build costs in exchange for long-term exclusive operating agreements. Zero capital at risk for our partners. Maximum strategic value for us.

It doesn't matter which AV company wins. Every autonomous fleet that operates in Canada needs what we've already built. That's the toll booth.

Where We Are

Active. In market.
Moving fast.

01

Vancouver as beachhead

BC leads Canada in EV adoption and has the most advanced AV regulatory framework. We're building our first exclusive infrastructure agreements here — with EasyPark, Impark, Indigo, and others already in conversation.

02

Parkade partnerships underway

We've identified and are actively engaging the key operators across Vancouver's high-density urban parkade network. Flock handles permitting, electrical, and installation — partners sign the exclusives.

03

Regulatory window is open

Canada's AV commercial approval is tracking within an 18-month horizon. The operators who sign infrastructure agreements now lock out competitors before the market materialises. That window doesn't stay open.

Canada's regulatory structure is a genuine advantage. Unlike the US, where AV rules fragment state-by-state, Canada's federal-provincial coordination creates a faster, cleaner path to commercial approval — and a more defensible first-mover position.
